Key Features

Electric Height Adjustment

The MITTZON features a dual-motor electric lifting system that provides smooth, quiet height adjustment between 27.5 and 47.2 inches. You can easily transition from sitting to standing positions with the touch of a button, and the motors handle the weight smoothly without jerking or stuttering.

The adjustment speed is reasonably quick, taking approximately 15-20 seconds to travel the full range. This makes frequent position changes practical and convenient throughout your workday.

Memory Presets

The desk includes four programmable memory presets that allow you to save your preferred sitting and standing heights. You simply adjust the desk to your desired position and hold down one of the numbered buttons until you hear a beep.

This feature eliminates the guesswork when switching positions and ensures consistent ergonomic positioning. The memory function works reliably and maintains settings even after power cycles.

Collision Detection

Safety is built into the MITTZON through its collision detection system, which automatically stops and reverses the desk movement if it encounters resistance. This protects both the desk mechanism and any objects that might be in the way during adjustment.

The sensitivity is well-calibrated, stopping movement when it encounters meaningful resistance while not being triggered by normal desktop items. This feature provides peace of mind, especially in busy home environments.

Sturdy Steel Frame

The desk utilizes a robust steel frame construction that provides excellent stability across the full height range. The T-shaped legs offer good lateral support, and you'll notice minimal wobbling even at maximum height.

Weight capacity is rated at 154 pounds, which accommodates multiple monitors, desktop computers, and typical office accessories. The frame feels solid during typing and doesn't transmit vibrations to the desktop surface.

Cable Management System

IKEA includes a basic cable management solution with a tray that mounts underneath the desktop. This helps keep power cords and data cables organized and prevents them from interfering with the desk's movement.

While not as sophisticated as some premium alternatives, the cable management system is functional and keeps your workspace tidy. The tray moves with the desk, ensuring cables don't get stretched or tangled during height adjustments.

Simple Control Panel

The control interface features a straightforward button layout with up/down arrows and four memory preset buttons. The display shows the current height in inches, making it easy to achieve consistent positioning.

The buttons have good tactile feedback and are clearly labeled. The control panel can be mounted on either side of the desk, accommodating both right and left-handed users.

Desktop Options

IKEA offers several desktop sizes and finishes to match different workspace needs and aesthetic preferences. Common options include rectangular tops in various wood finishes and sizes ranging from compact 47-inch widths to spacious 63-inch models.

The desktop surfaces are made from particleboard with melamine coating, providing a durable and easy-to-clean work surface. While not solid wood, the quality is consistent with IKEA's furniture standards.
