How long should you stand at a standing desk?

Experts recommend alternating between sitting and standing every 30-45 minutes. Start with shorter standing periods and gradually increase. ### Are standing desks worth the investment?

Yes. Studies show that standing desks can reduce back pain, improve posture, and boost energy levels throughout the workday. ### What is the ideal standing desk height?

Your elbows should be at a 90-degree angle when typing. For most people, this is between 38 and 46 inches.### Do standing desks help with back pain?

Many users report significant reduction in back pain after switching to a standing desk, especially when combined with proper ergonomics.## Conclusion
